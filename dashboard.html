Welcome to Taskmatrix

Taskmatrix is your personal dashboard to manage all your school assignments and class notifications. With Taskmatrix, you can:

  • Track and manage your assignments along with their due dates.
  • Keep detailed notes for each assignment to help you stay organized.
  • Set up and receive reminders for important class times and deadlines.
  • Organize all your academic activities in one centralized place.

Start by exploring the assignments or class reminder section to begin managing your tasks more efficiently.
